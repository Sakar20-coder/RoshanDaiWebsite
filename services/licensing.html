Business Licensing Services

We assist businesses in obtaining the necessary licenses and regulatory approvals required to operate legally in Nepal. Our team ensures a smooth, compliant, and hassle-free licensing process from start to finish.

Why Licensing is Important

  • Ensures full compliance with government regulations
  • Prevents legal penalties and business interruptions
  • Enhances credibility with customers and partners
  • Required for contracts, tenders, and expansion
  • Provides long-term operational security

Licenses We Assist With

Trade License

Mandatory license from local authorities to legally operate a business.

Tourism License

Required for travel agencies, trekking companies, and tourism services.

Import / Export License

Authorization for businesses involved in international trade.

Healthcare License

For clinics, hospitals, pharmacies, and medical institutions.

Education Consultancy License

Required for educational consulting and student placement services.

Industry Registration

For manufacturing, production, and industrial operations.

Our Licensing Process

1. Consultation

Ongoing assistance to maintain licenses and meet regulatory requirements.
